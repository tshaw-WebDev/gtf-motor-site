Why GTF Motor

QUALITY SUPPLIES

At GTF Motor we use only high-quality consumables supplied locally from companies such as General Traffic, GSF, Eurocarparts and NAPA for servicing your vehicles, aiming for fast but reliable repairs.

EXPERT MECHANICS

Our mechanics undergo rigorous training before handling customer cars.

OEM AUTO PARTS

Where possible we use OEM spec replacement parts straight from manufacturers like BMW, Mercedes,TPS, Ford for example. When OEM replacements are not possible, we turn to the best quality aftermarket alternatives for example, LUK, Febi, Durid, Valeo, Sachs etc.
